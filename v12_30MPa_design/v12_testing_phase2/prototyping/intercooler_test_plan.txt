================================================================
 INTERCOOLER CORE – PROTOTYPE TESTING PLAN
================================================================

## 1. OBJECTIVE
Validate heat‑transfer performance and pressure drop of the water‑cooled plate‑fin
intercooler core under simulated hypercar operating conditions.

## 2. PROTOTYPE SPECIFICATIONS
- **Core dimensions:** 400 × 400 × 100 mm (placeholder)
- **Fin type:** Offset‑strip fins (aluminum)
- **Fin density:** 20 fins/inch (7.87 fins/cm)
- **Plate thickness:** 0.3 mm
- **Material:** 3003‑H14 aluminum (brazable)
- **Design heat load:** 193 kW total (two cores)
- **Air‑side pressure drop target:** <5 kPa at 30 m/s face velocity

## 3. MANUFACTURING STEPS

### 3.1 CNC Machining of End‑Tanks & Headers
- **Material:** 6061‑T6 aluminum
- **Operations:** 3‑axis milling of inlet/outlet ports, O‑ring grooves
- **Quantity:** 2 sets (air side, coolant side)

### 3.2 Fin Stack Preparation
- **Fin stock:** Pre‑corrugated 3003‑H14 sheets
- **Cutting:** Laser cut to 398 × 398 mm
- **Cleaning:** Alkaline degrease, acid etch, rinse, dry

### 3.3 Vacuum Brazing
- **Furnace:** Vacuum furnace with temperature uniformity ±5 °C
- **Brazing alloy:** AWS BAISI‑4 (Al‑Si‑Mg) foil, 0.1 mm thickness
- **Cycle:** 
  1. Ramp to 450 °C at 10 °C/min (hold 30 min)
  2. Ramp to 600 °C at 5 °C/min (hold 60 min)
  3. Cool to 300 °C at 5 °C/min, then furnace cool
- **Post‑braze:** Pressure test at 1.5× design pressure (2.25 bar air‑side, 6 bar coolant‑side)

### 3.4 Finishing
- **Leak test:** Submerge in water, pressurize with air to 1.5 bar, inspect bubbles
- **Surface finish:** Mill flatness ≤0.1 mm, deburr ports
- **Coating:** None (bare aluminum)

## 4. TEST SETUP

### 4.1 Air Loop
- **Blower:** Centrifugal fan with VFD, 0–5000 m³/h capacity
- **Heater:** 30 kW electric air heater (PID controlled)
- **Ducting:** 400 × 400 mm square‑to‑round transition, flow straightener
- **Measurements:**
  - **Flow rate:** Orifice plate (ISO 5167) ±1%
  - **Temperature:** Type‑K thermocouples (air in/out) ±0.5 °C
  - **Pressure drop:** Differential pressure transducer (0–10 kPa) ±0.1% FS

### 4.2 Coolant Loop
- **Pump:** Centrifugal pump with bypass valve, 0–20 L/min
- **Chiller:** Recirculating chiller set to 20 °C (simulate radiator outlet)
- **Measurements:**
  - **Flow rate:** Magnetic flowmeter ±0.5%
  - **Temperature:** RTD probes (coolant in/out) ±0.1 °C
  - **Pressure:** Gauge pressure transducers (0–10 bar)

### 4.3 Data Acquisition
- **DAQ:** National Instruments cDAQ‑9189 with thermocouple & analog modules
- **Sampling rate:** 1 Hz (steady‑state), 10 Hz (transient)
- **Software:** LabVIEW custom VI (log T, ΔP, flow)

## 5. TEST MATRIX

| Run | Air velocity [m/s] | Air inlet temp [°C] | Coolant flow [L/min] | Coolant inlet temp [°C] | Purpose |
|-----|-------------------|---------------------|----------------------|-------------------------|---------|
| 1   | 10 | 35 | 10 | 20 | Baseline low‑flow |
| 2   | 20 | 35 | 10 | 20 | Mid‑flow |
| 3   | 30 | 35 | 10 | 20 | Design condition |

| 7   | 40 | 35 | 10 | 20 | Over‑speed condition |

## 6. EXPECTED RESULTS
- **Heat transfer rate:** ≥90 kW per core at design condition (30 m/s, ΔT_air=65 K)
- **Air‑side pressure drop:** ≤5 kPa at 30 m/s
- **Coolant‑side pressure drop:** ≤0.5 bar at 10 L/min
- **Effectiveness:** ≥0.85 (air‑side)

## 7. ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
- No leaks at 1.5× design pressure (2.25 bar air, 6 bar coolant)
- Heat‑transfer rate within ±10% of CFD prediction
- Pressure drop within ±15% of CFD prediction
- Thermal gradient across core face ≤5 K

## 8. TIMELINE
- **Week 1:** CNC machining of end‑tanks, laser cutting of fins
- **Week 2:** Brazing & pressure test
- **Week 3:** Instrumentation & test setup calibration
- **Week 4:** Steady‑state performance mapping
- **Week 5:** Transient response tests, data analysis, report

## 9. RISKS & MITIGATION
- **Risk:** Brazing voids reduce heat transfer.
  **Mitigation:** X‑ray inspection of sample coupons before full core.
- **Risk:** Air‑flow maldistribution due to duct transitions.
  **Mitigation:** Install flow straightener (honeycomb) upstream.
- **Risk:** Thermocouple conduction errors.
  **Mitigation:** Use thin‑wire exposed‑junction T/Cs, radiation shields.

## 10. DELIVERABLES
1. **Test report** with raw data, reduced performance curves, uncertainty analysis.
2. **Validated CFD boundary conditions** (heat‑transfer coefficient vs. velocity).
3. **Recommendations** for production core design (fin density, material thickness).
4. **As‑built drawings** of prototype with dimensional deviations.
